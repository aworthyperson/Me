Hi there! I am a 4th year Civil Engineering (Data Science Option) PhD student at the University of Washington. I spend most of my working time as a energy infrastructure researcher, educator, and data scientist. I am particularly interested in the interconnectivities between the built environment and natural world. That is, my research assesses building energy performance and its relationships to urban climate; aiming to address existing modeling gaps through combining more accurate environmental data into building energy models.

I hold an M.S. degree in Civil Engineering from the University of Washington, Seattle (2021) and B.S in Environmental Engineering from the University of Colorado, Boulder (2020).

I am always looking for new collaborators. Please feel free to reach out by email or over linkedin!
