Hi there! I am a 4th-year Civil Engineering PhD student with a Data Science option at the University of Washington. I spend most of my time working as an energy infrastructure researcher, educator, and data scientist. My primary interest is in making the built environment more sustainable through data-driven, performance-based analysis. Specifically, my research assesses the impact of urban climates on building energy performance; and aims aims to address existing modeling gap through incorperating more accurate environmental data into building energy models.

I hold an M.S. degree in Civil Engineering from the University of Washington, Seattle (2021) and B.S in Environmental Engineering from the University of Colorado, Boulder (2020).

I am always looking for new collaborators. Please feel free to reach out by email or over linkedin!
